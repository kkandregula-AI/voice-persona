# Voice Persona AI — Demo Explainer Video Script

---

## Product Summary

Voice Persona AI is a mobile-first PWA built with Expo and React Native that lets anyone clone their own voice in 15 seconds and use it to generate spoken content, translate conversations in real time, and interpret live speech into multiple languages simultaneously — all from a single app, no studio required.

---

## Target Audiences

- **Content Creators & Influencers** — Generate Reels, podcasts, and news scripts in their own voice without recording every word
- **Travelers & Tourists** — Communicate across language barriers with live two-way translation and big-text "show them" cards
- **Professionals & Public Speakers** — Practice speeches and presentations in different tones and persona modes
- **Multilingual Families & Communities** — Bridge everyday conversations across Indian and International languages simultaneously
- **Educators & Coaches** — Produce voice-narrated content quickly and consistently at scale

---

## CTA One-Liner

> **Try it free — no download needed:** voice-persona-gen.replit.app

---

## Key Features (One-Liners)

- Clone your voice in 15 seconds with ElevenLabs and use it everywhere in the app
- Choose from 3 persona modes — Natural, News Anchor, Storytelling — with 4 emotion layers
- Generate AI scripts from any topic and hear them spoken aloud in your own cloned voice
- Translate speech in real time across 30+ languages with two-way voice output
- Speak once and get simultaneous translations into multiple languages with per-language Speak buttons
- Full playback history with one-tap replay, download, and search

### Screen 2 — Voice Studio

**Screen Name:** Voice Studio

**Voice Over:**
"Voice Studio is where your voice lives. Record a short 15-second voice sample and the app uses ElevenLabs to clone it instantly. ### Screen 3 — Creator Mode

**Screen Name:** Creator Mode

**Voice Over:**
"Creator Mode is your AI script writing assistant. Just type a topic — anything from 'Morning motivation tips' to 'How to travel on a budget' — and the AI generates a ready-to-speak script for you. Pick your content format, choose a tone, and generate. The app speaks the finished script in your cloned voice."

### Screen 4 — Travel Talk

**Screen Name:** Travel Talk

**Voice Over:**
"Heading abroad? Travel Talk makes every conversation effortless. Pick your language and the other person's language, then tap Speak Out and talk. The app translates your speech instantly and speaks it aloud in their language. They respond, you switch to Listen Back, and it flows both ways. No squinting at tiny text — the translation is displayed in a big, bold card they can read at a glance."

**Features on this Screen:**
- Two-way language selector with 30+ languages — set 'My Language' and 'Their Language' and swap instantly
- Speak Out mode — tap the mic, speak, and your words are translated and spoken aloud in the target language
- Listen Back mode — the other person speaks, the app transcribes and translates back to your language
- Quick Translate panel for typed messages and Quick Travel Phrases for common situations like hotels, menus, and emergencies

---

### Screen 5 — Live Interpreter

**Screen Name:** Live Interpreter

**Voice Over:**
"Live Interpreter is built for multilingual moments — conferences, family gatherings, group tours, international meetings. Speak once and the app instantly translates your words into every language you've selected at the same time. Each language gets its own translation card with a dedicated Speak button so anyone in the room can hear their language read aloud."

**Features on this Screen:**
- Multi-target language selector — choose any combination from 30+ languages grouped by Indian and International regions
- Simultaneous translation — one spoken input produces live translation cards for all selected languages in parallel
- Per-language Speak button on every card — tap to hear the translation read aloud in that language via the device's speech engine
- 10-second rolling audio window with a live countdown timer — optimised for iOS Safari's MediaRecorder API

---

### Screen 6 — History

**Screen Name:** History

**Voice Over:**
"Every piece of content you generate is automatically saved to your History. Tap the play button to replay any entry on the spot. Search by title, filter by keyword, and delete what you no longer need. If you've connected ElevenLabs, your generated audio files are available to download directly from here."

**Features on this Screen:**

- One-tap replay button on every entry for instant audio playback without re-generating
- Search and filter bar to find any past entry by title or content keyword
- Delete individual entries or clear all history — ElevenLabs users also get a direct download option for each file
